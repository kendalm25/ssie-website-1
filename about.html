Who Are We?

We are a small group of students who are involved in the entertainment industry. Our goal is to make this industry
visible at Stanford, and help students remain passionate about the arts as a career path.

Calvin Laughlin

President

Calvin Laughlin is a senior serving as the President of SSIE. He's from Los Angeles and studies Computer Science and Art History. He loves all aspects of entertainment, especially audio entertainment such as podcasts and music, but he's also very interested in the intersection of entertainment and technology (specifically visual computing). He's very excited to begin this next year, and hopes to establish SSIE as Stanford’s premier entertainment club through increased programming and a more active presence on campus.

Calvin's Top 3's are:
Films: Fantastic Mr. Fox, Blade Runner, and Lost in Translation
TV Shows: The Offer, Ted Lasso, Black Mirror
Albums: Random Access Memories by Daft Punk, Wish You Were Here by Pink Floyd, and Flower Boy by Tyler, The Creator
