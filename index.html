Professional Expertise

Our community includes professionals who have worked at Netflix, Disney, HBOMax, Paramount, Universal Music Group, amongst other companies. Networking and mentorship are major aspects of our organization.

Events and Conferences

Apply to our annual Spring Break trip to attend the most significant conferences and events on and off campus. This includes the GSB Spring Conference, behind the scenes access to campus arts events, and more.

Community

Besides networking and professional events such as workshops, we will host social mixers to raise awareness of the talented student body.

Apply for our 2023-2024 SSiE Core Team

While all students will still be able to attend our general mixers, the SSiE core team, will have access to additional speaker and networking events, including mixers with our GSB and Law School counterpart organizations, AME and SESLA, alumni relationship building with members of SSiE’s postgraduate counterpart, Stanford in Entertainment, and social retreats and dinners to foster strong connections among the core team.
